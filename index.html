About G.C.E.S.I.

G.C.E.S.I. is a nonprofit initiative designed to address green workforce shortages in Greater Moncton. We connect people, skills, and materials in one integrated hub to create good jobs, reduce waste, and strengthen the local circular economy.

Powering a Circular Green Future

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission
To help people and communities in Greater Moncton create lasting green jobs and a strong circular economy through accessible skills training and business support.
Vision
A Greater Moncton where everyone can access good green jobs, businesses operate circularly, and communities thrive while protecting the environment.
Values
Equity & Inclusion · Environmental Stewardship · Community Collaboration · Innovation & Adaptation

What We Do

Skills Training
Hands-on training in solar, energy efficiency, sustainable agriculture, repair, and recycling.
Business Incubation
Support for circular startups through mentorship, space, equipment, and market access.
Material Recovery Hub
Keeping reusable materials in the community and out of landfill.
Community Engagement
Repair cafés, workshops, and public events that build practical skills and behavior change.

Governance & Leadership

G.C.E.S.I. is governed by a skills-based Board of Directors (9–11 members) focused on strategy, policy, and oversight. The organization follows Canadian nonprofit governance best practices, including fiduciary duties of care, loyalty, and obedience.
